Notification

This website is currently at draft Beta stage for consultation and feedback.

Frequent modifications are being made to data and content and users are advised not to include website data in publications until Version 1.00 is released. Registered users will receive updates about timing of releases. To become a registered user, and to use various features of the website not available to casual visitors, please login (see right hand side top banner).

The authors welcome constructive comments and details of errors or omissions via the feedback form (see the bottom banner of all pages).

While in Beta phase, the website will be taken offline periodically for modifications. Wherever possible, warning will be given in advance via this notification popup.
